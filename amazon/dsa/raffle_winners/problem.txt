An e-conmerce platform wants to implement a promotional raffle system
for a major sales event. Customers earn raffle entries based on their
total spending during the promotional period. The probability of winning
should be proportional to spending: a customer who spent $50 should have
5x the chance of winning compared to someone who spent $10. Write a
function that takes a list of transactions (customer_id, amount) and
returns K unique winners.
