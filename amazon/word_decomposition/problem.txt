Word Decomposition

You are given a (potentially large) list of words.

Some of these are compound words - the word can be broken down into parts and all parts of the word are also in the list.

Identify all combinations where one word can be composed of two or more words of the same list, and print them.

Example 1:
    Sample input:
        ["bar", "foobar", "foo",  "foobarzaa"]

    Sample output:
        "foobar": ["foo", "bar"]


Example 2:
    Sample input:
        ["high", "super", "way", "superhighway", "highway", "rockstar", "rock", "star", "rocks", "tar", "stars", "rockstars"]

    Sample output:
        "superhighway": ["super", "high", "way"]
        "superhighway": ["super", "highway"]
        "highway": ["high", "way"]
        "rockstar": ["rock", "star"]
        "rockstar": ["rocks", "tar"]
        "rockstars": ["rock", "stars"]
